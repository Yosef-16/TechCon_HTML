Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in San Francisco. What started as a single-track event has grown into one of the world's premier tech conferences, now attracting over 10,000 attendees annually. Over the years, we've expanded to multiple locations worldwide while maintaining our commitment to cutting-edge content and community building.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to bridge the gap between technology innovators and the broader community. We strive to create an inclusive environment where knowledge is shared freely, collaborations are born, and the future of technology is shaped through open dialogue and hands-on learning.

Core Principles

  • Foster innovation through knowledge sharing
  • Promote diversity in tech
  • Connect industry leaders with emerging talent
  • Showcase practical applications of emerging technologies

Past Notable Speakers

Dr. Sarah Chen

Artificial Intelligence pioneer and founder of NeuroTech AI, Dr. Chen's 2022 keynote on "Ethical AI Development" set new standards for responsible innovation in the field.

Jamal Williams

Lead developer at VirtuReal, Jamal transformed how we think about immersive experiences with his groundbreaking 2021 workshop on next-generation VR interfaces.

Priya Patel

Cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet, Priya's 2023 presentation on "Quantum-Resistant Cryptography" earned her TechCon's Innovator of the Year award.
